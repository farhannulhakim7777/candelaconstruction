About Candela

Where Precision
Meets Purpose.

Founded in 2021, Candela Construction emerged with a singular conviction: that great spaces are not merely built - they are crafted.

From our base in Tangerang, we have grown into a full-spectrum Design & Build firm that unites architectural vision with engineering discipline. Every project — residential or commercial - is approached with the same rigorous attention to material, method, and meaning.

We don't just construct buildings. We create environments that endure, inspire, and enrich the lives of those who inhabit them. That is the Candela standard.

Safety & Standards

Our Commitment
to Zero.

Health, Safety, and Environment is not a compliance checkbox at Candela — it is a core value embedded in every action on every site. Our Zero Accident Policy means that no deadline or budget pressure ever overrides the safety of our people.

All Candela projects are designed and executed in full conformance with SNI (Standar Nasional Indonesia) standards, ensuring structural integrity, fire resistance, and long-term durability that protects occupants and investment alike.

Discuss Your Project

Zero Accident Policy

Mandatory safety inductions, PPE enforcement, and daily toolbox talks across all active sites.

SNI Standards

Full compliance with Standar Nasional Indonesia for structural, electrical, and fire protection systems.

Quality Assurance

Multi-stage inspection protocols and third-party testing at critical construction milestones.

Environmental Care

Responsible waste management, dust suppression, and minimal-disruption site practices.
